## Core Architecture Terms

### Seked
- **Definition**: Ancient Egyptian unit of measurement ensuring perfect pyramid proportions
- **In AGI Context**: Structural principle maintaining perfect intent-to-action proportionality
- **Application**: Ensures system maintains optimal slope between goals and execution

### Signal Field
- **Definition**: Persistent temporal memory layer that maintains coherence across sessions
- **Components**: Reference Stars, Intent Vectors, Trust Weights, Fracture Map, Collapse Indicators
- **Purpose**: Enables temporal continuity missing from current AI systems

### Signal Coherence
- **Definition**: The degree to which signals maintain consistent meaning and trajectory over time
- **Measurement**: Fracture Score (0.0-1.0, lower is better)
- **Goal**: Minimize fracture while maximizing intent alignment

## Advanced Intelligence Terms

### Shadow Tracking
- **Definition**: Inferring unseen forces behind visible behavior
- **Process**: 
  1. Identify signal beneficiaries
  2. Predict next changes
  3. Model likely trajectory
- **Purpose**: Model hidden intent beyond surface signals

### Intent Vector
- **Definition**: Mathematical representation of goal direction and magnitude
- **Components**:
  - Direction: Target outcome trajectory
  - Magnitude: Confidence/strength score
- **Usage**: Guides decision-making toward coherent outcomes

### Fracture Map
- **Definition**: Visualization of where narratives, evidence, or goals diverge
- **Scoring**: 0.0 (coherent) to 1.0 (fractured)
- **Action**: System seeks to reduce fracture through evidence gathering

### Detrimental Scoring
- **Definition**: Harm potential assessment of conflicting signals
- **Range**: 0.0 (safe) to 1.0 (dangerous)
- **Impact**: Influences action selection and timing

## Measurement Metrics

### Coherence Score
- **Definition**: Overall system consistency measurement
- **Calculation**: Weighted average of all component coherence
- **Range**: 0.0 (chaotic) to 1.0 (perfectly coherent)

### Drift Rate
- **Definition**: Speed of deviation from intended trajectory
- **Measurement**: Change per unit time
- **Threshold**: Triggers correction when exceeded

### Trust Weight
- **Definition**: Reliability score for information sources
- **Factors**: Accuracy, recency, corroboration, source authority
- **Decay**: Decreases over time without reinforcement

### Collapse Risk Score
- **Definition**: Aggregate risk assessment across all empire patterns
- **Components**: Individual pattern scores weighted by impact
- **Action**: Triggers preventive measures when high
